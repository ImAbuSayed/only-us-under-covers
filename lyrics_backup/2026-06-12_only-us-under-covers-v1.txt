[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
Close the blinds and let the shadow fall
Your silhouette is painted on the wall
I hear your breath against the quiet night
Everything we are feels so right

[Verse 1]
We left the noise out on the highway miles
Traded the rush for just a little while
A wooden floor and a flicker of a flame
Whispering the way you say my name

[Pre-Chorus]
The world outside is spinning out of view
It all comes down to what I feel for you
The clock is frozen and the room is warm
I want to be your shelter from the storm

[Chorus]
It is only us under the covers now
Falling into deep and leaning into how
Your skin is melting right against my own
This is the greatest fire I have known

[Post-Chorus]

(Under covers) Where we go

[Verse 2]
